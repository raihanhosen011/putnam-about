Why We Stand Behind Our Work

The work you cannot see is the work that matters most.

The prep we never charge extra for. The extra coat we apply without asking. The corners we caulk where nobody will check. That is where a real paint job is built.

We offer a 5-Year Workmanship Warranty because we already do the work that earns it. The warranty is not a marketing line. It is a written commitment that what we did when nobody was watching will hold up for years to come.

If our workmanship fails within five years, we come back. We fix it. We do not argue about it. That is the standard Meghan and Jordan built this company on, and it is the standard every member of our team holds themselves to.

Coverage Areas

What Our Warranty Covers

If any of the following happens within five years of project completion due to our workmanship, we come back and fix it at zero cost to you.

Paint Peeling or Flaking

If paint lifts, flakes, or peels from a properly prepared surface, we return to scrape, re-prime, and repaint the affected area.

Cracking or Splitting

Paint cracks or splits from a properly prepped surface within five years are covered under our workmanship guarantee.

Surface Preparation Failures

Any failure traceable to our prep work — improper sanding, missed primer, inadequate caulking — is corrected on us.

Cabinet Refinish Failures

Cabinet refinishing that chips, peels, or fails due to our application is fully covered including all touch-up materials.

Coastal Climate Failure

Premature fading, blistering, or weather-related failure on properly prepped exteriors near the Maine coast is on us to correct.

Drywall Repair Failures

Skim coat or drywall repair that cracks, sags, or fails within the warranty period is corrected at no charge to you.

Clear and Honest

What's In. What's Out.

We believe in clarity. Here is exactly what our warranty covers and what falls outside it. No fine print, no gotchas.

What's Covered

  • Premature paint peeling, flaking, or blistering
  • Cracking, splitting, or checking of paint film
  • Application defects from our team's workmanship
  • Surface preparation failures we are responsible for
  • Cabinet refinishing finish failures
  • Drywall and skim coat repair failures
  • Caulking joint failures we applied
  • Color uniformity issues from our application
  • Free touch-ups and repaints for any covered issue

What's Not Covered

  • Damage from extreme weather events (storms, hurricanes)
  • Damage caused by other contractors or homeowners
  • Normal wear and tear over time
  • Pre-existing structural issues (settling, water damage)
  • Surfaces we explicitly excluded in the original estimate
  • Damage from cleaning with harsh chemicals or abrasives
  • Color fade beyond manufacturer paint specifications
  • Furniture, fixture, or landscape damage caused by clients
  • Work modified or altered after our completion

Warranty Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Straight answers about our 5-Year Workmanship Warranty and how it protects your investment.

How long is the warranty valid?
Our Workmanship Warranty is valid for 5 full years from the date your project is completed. The warranty period begins the day we finish the final walkthrough with you.
Is the warranty transferable if I sell my home?
Yes. Our warranty transfers automatically to new homeowners for the remaining coverage period. Just provide the new owner with your original project paperwork. No paperwork or fees required from us.
Do I need to keep paperwork for the warranty?
We strongly recommend it. We keep your full project records on file as well, but having your own copy speeds up the claim process. Every customer receives a written warranty certificate at project completion.
What if the paint manufacturer's warranty conflicts with yours?
Our workmanship warranty covers our application. Paint products themselves are covered separately by Sherwin-Williams, Benjamin Moore, or Johnson Paint manufacturer warranties. If a paint product fails, we help you file with the manufacturer at no charge.
Will you come back for small touch-ups under warranty?
Yes. We do not have minimum thresholds for warranty work. If something we did is not holding up, we come back regardless of how minor it seems. Your reputation with your neighbors matters to us.
What if I have not maintained the paint properly?
Reasonable use is expected. Standard cleaning with gentle products is fine. Pressure-washing exterior paint within the first year, scrubbing interior paint with abrasive chemicals, or otherwise damaging the surface may void specific warranty claims. We will assess case by case.
How quickly do you respond to warranty claims?
We inspect every warranty claim within 7 business days of being contacted. Most repairs are scheduled within 2 to 4 weeks depending on weather and our current schedule. Urgent issues are prioritized.
Does the warranty cover commercial projects too?
Yes. Our 5-Year Workmanship Warranty applies to both residential and commercial painting projects across York County and southern Cumberland County.
