Chapter 01

How It
Started

2014
Year Established

Jordan did not set out to start a painting company.

He found his calling restoring old oceanfront homes along the Maine coast — homes that had been beaten by salt air, UV exposure, and a hundred freeze-and-thaw cycles a year. He saw what the right paint, applied the right way, could do for a weathered home. The craft pulled him in.

Every prep step became deliberate. Every product choice became intentional. Every brushstroke had to earn its place. That obsession became Putnam Coastal Painting.

"I truly love my work, and it shows in every project we undertake."

Meghan brought what Jordan could not. While Jordan refined the craft on the ladder, Meghan built the business behind it — answering calls, managing schedules, mastering cabinet refinishing as her own specialty, and making sure every client felt heard from the first phone call to the final walkthrough.

Together they grew slowly, project by project, referral by referral. No marketing tricks. No outsourcing. Just two people committed to a single standard: do the work right whether anyone is watching or not.
